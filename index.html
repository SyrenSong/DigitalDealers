The Digital Dealers Deck Shuffler

\tWhat comes to your mind when you think of playing cards? Poker? Rummy? I think of my grandparents playing War and Go Fish! with me as a kid. But as we get older we lose our fine motor abilities. Things that were once easy seem impossible. I don’t want to stop playing cards with my grandparents but what if I’m also unable to shuffle cards?

Introducing the Digital Dealers Deck Shuffler! This device automatically shuffles a deck of cards, and we used this as a base to also check the deck for duplicates and make sure it is a full deck. In our case a full deck consists of 52 cards with no duplicates and 2 jokers. Our group recognizes that people dont only play with a deck of playing cards, what if youre playing UNO. So we added a button to turn off the feature which scans for a full deck incase the user does not wish to use it.

Many people struggle with shuffling, it can be as simple as not getting the motion right. Our group aims to make it just a bit easier.

This button shuffles the deck. Simply press it and watch the deck shuffler shuffle your cards for you!

This button stops the program from checking if it is a full deck. If you aren't using a deck of cards or you dont care if your deck is full, press this button.
